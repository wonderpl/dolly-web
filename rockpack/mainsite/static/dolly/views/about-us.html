Team

Wonder PL is brought to you by a hard working, talented team of developers, creatives and film makers.

We are creating a media platform that is better, one that has the power to instruct, inspire and fill people’s lives with wonder.

We care about our content providers. To produce a quality platform requires a different mind set and orientation, down to every design decision through to winning editorial. We want them to flourish here and build meaningful connections with consumers and brands.

There is more to do, and we need your help to do it. Write to us with your suggestions, tell us what you think about the new designs and features, or just say hi. Get in touch with us at hello@wonderpl.com.

Partners

Here at Wonder PL, we have partnered with some of the world's biggest and best companies to bring you the services, tools and content you deserve.

 

No one wants their viewing experience ruined by banner ads and ugly overlays. With Ooyala, the world's premium provider of streaming services, we will provide a clean viewing experience. Ooyala powers sites such as ESPN and American Express.

Epidemic Sound is the world's largest collection of music for use in video production. One of the founders, Peer Anstrom, holds the world record for most entries on the US Billboard/iTunes charts.

You can use the complete Epidemic Sound catalog for free in your productions.

Culture Shock is a New York City-based creative agency recognised for pioneering approaches to art and technology.  We are working with them to provide on the edge art content and create a home where artists and film-makers can thrive.

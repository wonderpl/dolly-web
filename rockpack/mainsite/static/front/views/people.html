Sofia Fenichell
Chief Executive Officer
"I make ideas happen."

Sofia is a technology 'grown up'. In a career spanning 20 years, she has been at the forefront of the internet revolution, advising and investing in a number of global technology companies both large and small, including Nokia, Ericsson, Real Networks and Inktomi.

In 2006, she decided to 'go rogue' and apply her talents to the start–up scene. She co-founded her first company, Beetle Capital, and after exiting that business, worked in senior roles for a number of start-ups including State.com, a global opinion network founded by Alex Asseily, Chairman of Jawbone.

Prior to that, Sofia spent 10 years in finance as a technology investor and expert, working for UBS, Merrill Lynch, Morgan Stanley and Caxton – where she was voted Best Buyside Analyst across all sectors by Institutional Investor and Extel. Sofia spent four years as a senior account executive in advertising at DDB Needham working on global consumer brands.

She has an MBA from Columbia Business School and a BA in History from Northwestern University.
